  • About Us

    Welcome!

    We are a group of people dedicated to wildlife and awakening consciousness by connecting with nature and its living beings.

    We carry out guided tours of meditation, observation of wildlife, nature photography, sunsets and many more, in different parts of Puerto Vallarta, Jalisco, and Riviera Nayarit.

  • Mission

    Thank you for choosing us!

    Our mission is to create a bond between people and nature through the contemplation of free flora and fauna, as well as the exploration of natural areas abundant in wildlife with the purpose of awakening and expanding the collective conscience regarding conservation, restoration and protection of biodiversity and terrestrial and marine ecosystems.

    Share a unique experience of inner introspection through the interaction with the outside.

    Provide a service where empathy with nature and humans is a priority.

  • Vision

    More Programs to come!

    Our vision contemplates the expansion of human consciousness through direct experiences with nature and its lifeforms.

    It also means to enhance human health and well-being through alternative healing techniques, such as meditation in the open air, contemplation of nature, and conscious coexistence with wildlife.

    To be a leading company, not only in conscious tourism, but in the generation of awareness and introspection in people to generate a social change that has a positive impact on the planet and its ecosystems.
